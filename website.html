INTRODUCTION

Technology plays a transformative role in revolutionizing the design, construction, and operation of buildings and infrastructure. With its advancements, it has become a catalyst for enhancing decision-making and overall performance throughout the entire lifecycle of these structures. One such innovative approach is Building Information Modeling (BIM), which leverages intelligent 3D models to empower architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) professionals. By providing valuable insights and efficient tools, BIM enables them to effectively strategize, design, build, and manage buildings and infrastructure in a more streamlined and effective manner.

Transitioning into the field of research can be a daunting task, whether you come from a background in academia or industry. It requires careful consideration and guidance from experts worldwide to embark on this journey. This short-term training program aims to provide immense benefits to both aspiring and seasoned researchers. For newcomers, it serves as a valuable platform to dive into the realm of research and gain knowledge from experienced professionals, enabling them to stay up-to-date with the latest advancements and methodologies. Additionally, participants will develop a deeper understanding of how to establish their own brand value as researchers. This program also offers a unique opportunity for academicians to explore new research styles and expand their horizons. Regardless of specialization, undergraduate, postgraduate, PhD scholars, academicians, researchers, and professionals can all reap the rewards of this program. By attending lectures delivered by industry and academia experts, participants will gain a fresh perspective on various aspects of research, fostering their growth in this ever-evolving field.

TARGET GROUP

The session is expected to offer benefits to a wide range of participants, including undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) students, industry professionals with backgrounds in architecture and civil engineering, as well as faculty members from diverse engineering and architecture colleges/institutions.

The Department of Architecture is organizing a one-week hybrid national workshop on BIMAEC 2023, aimed at providing aspiring researchers with a unique opportunity to explore a wide range of study areas. This program, conducted under the Continuing Education Program (CEP) by the esteemed National Institute of Technology Calicut, holds significant value for individuals from diverse backgrounds, including architecture, civil engineering, and other related fields. BIMAEC 2023 is designed to cater to a diverse population of participants, ensuring that individuals from various educational and professional backgrounds can benefit from the workshop. Whether you are a student, industry professional, or faculty member, this program promises to offer valuable insights and a comprehensive understanding of different research areas. The workshop, scheduled from 22nd to 27th July 2023, will adopt a hybrid format, combining both in-person and online sessions to accommodate participants from different locations. By embracing this approach, BIMAEC 2023 aims to maximize accessibility and facilitate meaningful interactions among participants. As an institute of national importance under the Ministry of Education, Government of India, the National Institute of Technology Calicut is committed to promoting excellence in education and research. With their expertise and support, this workshop aims to provide a dynamic and enriching experience for all attendees, empowering them to make informed choices in their research pursuits. Whether you are an aspiring researcher looking to explore different study areas or a professional seeking to expand your knowledge, BIMAEC 2023 offers a platform for growth and discovery. Join us in this exciting journey of learning and collaboration as we delve into the fascinating world of architecture, civil engineering, and beyond.
